Industry Challenges

New Challenges Facing Utilities Today

Aging infrastructure, long material lead times, and pressure to deliver reliable service at lower costs

Siloed Legacy Systems

Disconnected applications force manual re-entry. A valve added in GIS takes days to sync with ERP, causing delays, errors, and no real-time visibility.

Aging Assets & Limited Maintenance

Critical infrastructure is deteriorating. Without predictive maintenance, utilities risk catastrophic events and service outages.

Inaccurate Demand Forecasting

New technologies and weather volatility make usage unpredictable. Failed schedules lead to expensive over- or under-production.

Regulatory Pressure & Audits

Rising regulatory pressure demands real-time reporting and audit readiness. Inadequate systems make compliance painful and slow.

Poor Field-Office Integration

Manual processes with paper work orders prevent instant updates. Data discrepancies snowball, hindering outage response.
